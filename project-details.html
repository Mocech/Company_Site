Church Event Flyer Design

Graphic Design November 2024

Project Overview

This project involved designing an elegant church conference flyer aimed at promoting a three-day community event. The design combines modern typography with meaningful messaging while maintaining the solemnity appropriate to the occasion. The flyer was created for both digital and print distribution.

Challenge

The client needed to increase attendance at their annual conference. They wanted a design that would stand out on social media and in print, while effectively communicating event details in a clear and professional manner.

Solution

We created a clean, hierarchical layout using the brand's color palette (navy and gold) to establish authority and elegance. Key information is prominent, while supporting details remain organized and easy to scan. The design was optimized for multiple formats and platforms.

Results

  • 2,000+ digital impressions in first week
  • 35% increase in conference attendance
  • Shared across 5+ social media platforms
  • Client requested additional design services
